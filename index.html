Landing Page

    Introduction

    A multi-section landing page, with a dynamically updating navigational menu based on the amount of content that is added to the page. The project contains many features, which are listed below.

    Hidden Elements

    To save space and maintain an attractive site, the project includes the feature of hiding certain elements, such as buttons and navigation bars. These elements will only appear when users scroll or hover their mouse over the area. The transition between hidden and visible states is smooth, providing an aesthetic aspect.
